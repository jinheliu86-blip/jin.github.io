公司简介 Company Overview

Overview

公司成立于 2007 年 5 月，是一家专注于国际货运代理和多式联运的综合物流服务商。公司提供全方位的物流服务，包括海运、空运、陆运及综合供应链解决方案，支持国内和国际货物运输。

Established in May 2007, the company is a comprehensive logistics service provider specializing in international freight forwarding and multimodal transportation. It provides a full range of logistics services including ocean freight, air freight, trucking, and integrated supply chain solutions, supporting both domestic and international cargo transportation.

综合物流服务 / Integrated Logistics

Covers ocean freight, air freight, trucking, and supporting supply chain services for diversified logistics scenarios.

国际货代能力 / Freight Forwarding Capability

国内外协同 / Domestic & Global Coordination

国际物流网络 Global Logistics Network

North America

WMWM UNITED INVESTMENT HOLDING INC., established in May 2020 in Canada, serves as the North American agency and logistics platform, focusing on freight coordination, trucking services, and inland distribution across the United States and Canada.
